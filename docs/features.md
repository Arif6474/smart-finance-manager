# Smart Finance Manager - Feature Directory

This document provides a detailed overview of the features available in the Smart Finance Manager application.

## 1. Dashboard
The Dashboard is your financial control center. It provides a real-time summary of your financial status:
- **Total Balance**: Aggregated balance across all your accounts.
- **Monthly Overview**: A quick look at your income vs. expenses for the current month.
- **Budget Tracking**: Visual progress bars showing how much of your monthly budget you've spent.
- **Recent Transactions**: A list of your latest financial activities.
- **AI Insights Widget**: A snapshot of the latest advice from your AI financial assistant.

## 2. Account Management
Manage all your money in one place.
- **Multiple Account Types**: Support for Bank accounts, Cash on hand, and Mobile Banking (e.g., bKash, Rocket).
- **Balance Tracking**: Each account maintains its own balance, which is updated automatically when you record transactions.
- **Currency Support**: Default currency is BDT, but the system is designed to handle multiple currencies.

## 3. Transaction Tracking
Record every penny that moves in or out.
- **Income & Expenses**: Easily categorize your spending (e.g., Food, Salary, Rent, Entertainment).
- **Transfers**: Move money between your accounts (e.g., withdrawing cash from your bank).
- **History & Filtering**: Search and filter your transaction history by date, category, or account.

## 4. Smart Budgeting
Stay disciplined with your spending.
- **Category-Based Budgets**: Set monthly limits for specific categories.
- **Real-time Monitoring**: As you add expenses, the system automatically tracks them against your active budgets.
- **Overspending Alerts**: Visual indicators warn you when you're close to or have exceeded your budget.

## 5. Financial Goals
Save for what matters.
- **Target Tracking**: Set a target amount and a deadline for your savings goals (e.g., "New Laptop", "Emergency Fund").
- **Progress Visualization**: See exactly how close you are to reaching your target.

## 6. AI Financial Assistant
Get personalized advice powered by AI.
- **Daily Insights**: The AI analyzes your transactions, budgets, and debts to provide tailored tips.
- **Success & Warnings**: Receive "success" badges for good habits and "warning" alerts for potential issues.
- **Financial Summary**: A human-readable summary of your current financial health.

## 7. Subscriptions & Recurring Payments
Never get surprised by a renewal.
- **Track Services**: Keep a list of your active subscriptions (e.g., Netflix, Gym, Internet).
- **Next Billing Date**: Know exactly when your next payment is due.
- **Cost Analysis**: See the total monthly cost of all your recurring services.

## 8. Reminders & Task Management
Stay on top of your financial to-dos.
- **Custom Reminders**: Set alerts for bill payments, rent, or any other financial task.
- **Priority Levels**: Categorize reminders by priority (Low, Medium, High).
- **Status Tracking**: Mark tasks as "Completed" or "Pending".

## 9. Payables & Receivables (Debt Management)
Track money you owe and money owed to you.
- **Lending & Borrowing**: Record debts with friends, family, or institutions.
- **Loan Support**: Specialized tracking for loans, including Principal, Interest Rate, and EMI calculations.
- **Automatic Transactions**: Creating a new debt entry can automatically trigger an account balance update.

## 10. Financial Health Score
Gamify your financial journey.
- **Score Calculation**: A dynamic score based on your savings habits, budget adherence, and debt management.
- **Score History**: Track how your financial health improves over time.

## 11. Reports & Analytics
Understand your patterns through data.
- **Visual Charts**: Interactive charts showing income vs. expense trends.
- **Category Breakdown**: See exactly where your money is going with pie charts.
- **Monthly Comparisons**: Analyze your performance month-over-month.

## 12. Account & Pro Membership
- **Secure Authentication**: JWT-based login with Google OAuth support.
- **Manual Payment Verification**: Request Pro upgrades via bKash, Nagad, or Bank Transfer with manual admin approval.
